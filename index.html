The Winning Woman

About the Book

The Winning Woman is more than a book — it’s a healing invitation. Written for women who are tired of pretending, tired of performing, and secretly bleeding behind their brilliance, this book is a personal conversation, a quiet confrontation, and a spiritual blueprint for wholeness.

Whether you're battling shame, rebuilding after betrayal, or simply longing for emotional rest, The Winning Woman walks beside you like a friend with open arms and honest words. Inside, you’ll find confessions, prayers, and raw reflections designed to restore the woman behind the smile.

About the Author

Toyin Bami-Daramola is a passionate minister, counselor, and women’s life strategist dedicated to emotional healing and spiritual restoration. With over a decade of mentoring women and leading healing spaces, Toyin writes not to impress, but to transform. Her words are honest, her stories unfiltered, and her mission unmistakable: to help every woman rise from brokenness into wholeness.
